# Privacy Policy for Qatrak
**Last updated: March 18, 2026**

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Location Data:** Qatrak collects precise and approximate location data to enable real-time train tracking. This data is collected even when the app is closed or not in use if you choose to share your location.
* **Account Information:** We use Google Sign-in to authenticate users. We collect your name and email address provided by Google.

## 2. How We Use Information
The location data is shared with other users of the app to show the live position of the train. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 3. Advertisements
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data for personalized advertising purposes.
